A little about me

As a young student, instead of taking notes about George Washington crossing the Delaware River, I'd doodle little storyboards instead. My teacher caught me — and instead of scolding me, suggested I pursue graphic design. Her advice changed my life.

Today I design at Google, where I bring that same creative instinct to products used by millions. Good design, to me, is invisible — it just feels right.
